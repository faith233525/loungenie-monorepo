What LounGenie is

LounGenie is a premium poolside hardware platform that goes into cabanas and premium seating areas, built around four pillars: ORDER, STASH, CHARGE, and CHILL. It is designed and built in North York, Ontario, Canada, and installed, maintained, and serviced by PoolSafe.
