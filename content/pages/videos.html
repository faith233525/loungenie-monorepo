LounGenie Videos & Walkthroughs

Video is one of the best ways to see how LounGenie actually feels on a live pool deck. This page is designed to host short, focused videos you can share with internal teams and stakeholders.

Suggested video sections

  • Overview – A quick walkthrough of ORDER, STASH, CHARGE, and CHILL and how the unit fits into a typical day at the pool.
  • Guest experience – How guests use the safe, call button, QR code, and charging ports during a visit.
  • Staff experience – How service calls and QR orders appear on staff tools and how they are fulfilled.
  • Property integration – How LounGenie units are customized to match towels, cabanas, and the overall property aesthetic.

The actual video embeds on this page can be managed in the WordPress editor. As you produce new footage or refine your story, you can easily reorder or replace videos without changing the rest of the site.
