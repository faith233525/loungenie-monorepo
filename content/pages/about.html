About LounGenie

LounGenie is a premium poolside hardware platform that lives inside your cabanas and premium seating areas. It is designed and built in North York, Ontario, Canada, and installed, maintained, and serviced by PoolSafe.

What LounGenie does for your property

  • ORDER – Guests scan a QR code to place food and beverage orders. Orders print on a dedicated PoolSafe printer at your service station, without forcing you to rebuild your existing POS.
  • STASH – A waterproof safe with a waterproof keypad, built into the unit, so guests can lock away phones, wallets, and keys and actually relax.
  • CHARGE – Solar‑powered USB charging ports so devices stay powered through a full day at the pool.
  • CHILL – A removable ice bucket that keeps bottles and cans close at hand, right at the lounger.
  • SERVICE – A guest‑facing service call button that alerts a staff‑facing touchscreen monitor when any cabana needs attention.

Three tiers that build on each other

LounGenie is available in three cumulative tiers. Each tier includes everything from the tier below.

  • Classic – STASH (safe) + CHARGE (solar USB) + CHILL (removable ice bucket).
  • Service+ – Classic plus a general "I need service" call button that alerts your staff touchscreen monitor.
  • 2.0 – Service+ plus QR ordering, so guests can scan and send orders that print to a dedicated PoolSafe printer.

How LounGenie fits into your operation

LounGenie is deployed directly into your existing cabanas and premium seating areas. You do not need to rebuild your POS or add new guest‑facing touchscreens. The platform is designed to work alongside your current service model and staffing levels, while increasing revenue per occupied seat.

  • Premium seating becomes a clearly defined upsell with a better, safer guest experience.
  • Service calls and QR orders are routed to staff tools (touchscreen monitor and printer) that fit into your current back‑of‑house workflows.
  • PoolSafe manages installation, maintenance, and ongoing service of each unit.
