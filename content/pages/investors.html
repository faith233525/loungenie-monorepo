This page is for investors and financial stakeholders who want to understand how LounGenie fits into the broader PoolSafe story and the premium hospitality space.

Why LounGenie exists

Premium poolside seating is one of the most valuable pieces of real estate on property, but it has traditionally been difficult to manage consistently. LounGenie was created to turn cabanas and premium seating into a reliable, guest‑friendly revenue engine built around four simple pillars: ORDER, STASH, CHARGE, and CHILL.

What investors should know

  • Clear value proposition – Better guest experience and higher revenue per occupied cabana or premium seat.
  • Operationally aware – Designed to fit into existing staffing and service models, rather than requiring a ground‑up rebuild.
  • Hardware + service – The platform combines purpose‑built hardware with ongoing installation, maintenance, and service from PoolSafe.
