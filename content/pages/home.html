How LounGenie fits into a day at the pool

1. Guests arrive

Guests are welcomed to a cabana or premium lounger that already has a LounGenie unit installed, fully charged, and ready.

2. Belongings go into the safe

They set a personal code on the waterproof safe, lock up phones, wallets, and keys, and finally stop “checking the chair” every few minutes.

3. Service and orders, on demand

The integrated call button alerts your team’s staff monitor when a cabana needs help. With LounGenie 2.0, guests can also scan a QR code to place food & beverage orders that print to a PoolSafe printer at your service station.

4. More revenue, less friction

Premium seating becomes a predictable revenue stream: more ticket upgrades, more F&B per occupied cabana, and higher guest satisfaction scores.
