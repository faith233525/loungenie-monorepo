LounGenie Features

LounGenie is a premium poolside platform that goes into your cabanas and premium seating areas. It is designed to make every visit more convenient for guests and more profitable for your property.

The four core features

  • ORDER – Guests scan a QR code at the lounger to place food and beverage orders. Orders print on a dedicated PoolSafe printer at your service station instead of forcing a full POS rebuild.
  • STASH – A waterproof safe with a waterproof keypad built into the unit, so guests can lock away phones, wallets, and keys while they enjoy the water.
  • CHARGE – Solar‑powered USB charging ports that keep phones and small devices powered through the entire day.
  • CHILL – A removable ice bucket at the lounger to keep bottles and cans close at hand.

Service and operations

LounGenie is built to work with your existing staffing and service model.

  • Service call button – Guests press a single button on the unit when they need help. Alerts appear on a staff‑facing touchscreen monitor showing which cabana or lounger needs attention.
  • QR ordering workflow – For LounGenie 2.0, QR orders are printed on a PoolSafe printer at your service station, so your team can route and fulfill them using your current processes.
  • Flexible placement – Units are installed directly into your cabanas and premium seating, so they feel like part of the property, not a separate kiosk.

Designed for your property

  • Custom colors and finishes to match your towels, cabanas, and overall aesthetic.
  • Branding options for logos and on‑unit messaging.
  • Commercial‑grade construction with a solid interior body designed for outdoor environments.

Frequently asked questions about LounGenie

Does LounGenie connect directly to our POS system?

No. LounGenie uses a dedicated PoolSafe printer to output guest QR orders at your service station. Your team can key those tickets into your existing POS, so you do not have to rebuild or replace your current system.

What comes with each LounGenie unit?

Every LounGenie deployment includes the core hardware at the seat: a waterproof safe with waterproof keypad, solar‑powered USB charging ports, and a removable ice bucket. Depending on the tier you choose, you can add the service call button and QR ordering functions on top of that foundation.

Can we customize the colors and branding?

Yes. LounGenie is designed to match your property. Units can be ordered in colors that align with your towels, cabanas, and lounge chairs, and you can add logos or on‑unit branding to reinforce your visual identity.

What kinds of properties is LounGenie built for?

LounGenie is built for hotels, resorts, and waterparks that operate cabanas, daybeds, and other premium seating zones. It is especially effective wherever you want a more modern guest experience and stronger revenue performance from high‑value seats.

How is LounGenie installed and serviced?

PoolSafe handles installation, maintenance, and service for the units. Your team does not take on new hardware support, and there is no IT rebuild required. The commercial model is based on a revenue‑share approach instead of a large upfront capital purchase.
