LounGenie Financials & Investment Overview

LounGenie is part of the PoolSafe family of products. This page is intended for financial stakeholders who want to understand how LounGenie can impact revenue, guest satisfaction, and long‑term value at the property level.

Revenue at the cabana level

  • Premium seating uplift – Cabanas and daybeds become clearly differentiated, higher‑value experiences anchored by the LounGenie platform.
  • Food & beverage – QR ordering and easier service can help increase F&B captured from each occupied premium seat.
  • Ancillary revenue – Branding, sponsorship, and seasonal programming around the units can add incremental opportunity.

Operational efficiency

  • Better signal, less guesswork – Service calls and QR orders are routed through staff tools so teams know exactly which cabana needs attention.
  • Fits existing workflows – The platform is designed to complement current staffing levels and back‑of‑house processes instead of replacing them.
