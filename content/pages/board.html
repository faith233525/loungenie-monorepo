LounGenie Board & Leadership Overview

LounGenie is part of the PoolSafe family of products, backed by experienced leadership in hospitality technology, manufacturing, and operations. This page provides high‑level information about the board and leadership team that guide the vision for LounGenie.

What our board focuses on

  • Guest experience – Ensuring LounGenie delivers a better, safer poolside experience for guests in cabanas and premium seating.
  • Operational excellence – Making sure the platform fits smoothly into existing staffing, service, and F&B workflows.
  • Responsible growth – Expanding into new properties and regions in a way that protects brand standards and guest satisfaction.
  • Innovation – Continuing to invest in features such as QR ordering, service call routing, and property‑friendly customization.

Leadership priorities

The leadership team behind LounGenie and PoolSafe is focused on three simple outcomes:

  1. Make premium seating more profitable. Turn cabanas and daybeds into reliable revenue engines, not just amenities.
  2. Make service easier for teams. Give staff the tools they need to respond faster to guest needs, without adding complexity to their day.
  3. Protect the guest relationship. Build hardware and experiences that feel on‑brand for each property and earn repeat visits.
