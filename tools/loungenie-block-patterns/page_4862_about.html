About LounGenie

LounGenie provides premium poolside technology to enhance guest comfort and boost F&B revenue through QR ordering, secure waterproof storage, and solar USB charging.

Our Tiers

  • Classic — STASH (waterproof safe) + CHARGE (USB charging) + CHILL (removable ice bucket)
  • Service+ — Classic + service call button (staff touchscreen alerts)
  • 2.0 — Service+ + QR ordering (prints order on dedicated printer)

All units include a waterproof safe with keypad and commercial-grade construction. LounGenie units are customizable to match property aesthetics.
