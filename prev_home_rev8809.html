The Core Problem

Your Pool Deck Is Leaking Revenue Every Hour.

Every pool deck loses revenue the same three ways. Guests leave to charge phones. They skip food orders because no server is nearby. They cut their stay short because they do not trust leaving valuables on a lounge chair. Every hour a guest walks off the deck is a missed food and beverage opportunity. The LounGenie solves all three problems at the guest’s seat.

How It Works

One Unit. Four Features. Higher Spend Per Guest.

Order

Order at the seat — no app required.

Guests scan a QR code to order food and drinks — the order prints on a dedicated printer provided by PoolSafe. A service call button alerts staff via a touchscreen monitor showing exactly which cabana needs attention. No app download. No POS integration. No added labor.

Stash

A waterproof safe guests actually use.

Guests lock phones, wallets, and room keys inside the cabana with a waterproof keypad safe. When valuables are secure, guests stay longer and spend more. STASH is consistently the feature guests mention most in reviews.

Charge

Solar-powered charging — no wiring, no permits.

Solar panels power USB charging from sunrise to sunset at no electricity cost. When guests can charge poolside, they stop leaving the deck. And when they stay, they order.

Chill

Cold drinks at arm’s reach drive repeat orders.

A removable ice bucket keeps drinks cold at the seat for hours. Cold beverages within arm’s reach encourage high-margin repeat orders throughout the afternoon — without a server needing to check in.
