About LounGenie

LounGenie brings premium cabana technology to hospitality venues — waterproof safe, solar USB charging, removable ice bucket, service call button, and optional QR ordering.

Our Story

Built for commercial poolside and cabana environments, LounGenie is designed to be installed and serviced by PoolSafe.

Core Features

  • Waterproof safe with keypad
  • Solar USB charging ports
  • Removable ice bucket (CHILL)
  • Service call button
  • Optional QR ordering (prints to staff printer)
